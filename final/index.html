About us

Welcome to Andean Roots, your gateway to the rich cultural heritage of Ecuador. We specialize in authentic Ecuadorian handicrafts, offering a diverse range of products that showcase the artistry and traditions of the Andean region. Our collection includes handmade textiles, pottery, jewelry, and more, all crafted by skilled artisans who pour their heart and soul into every piece.

At Andean Roots, we are committed to supporting local communities and preserving traditional craftsmanship. Each purchase you make helps sustain these artisans and their families, allowing them to continue their work and pass down their skills to future generations.

Explore our selection of unique and beautiful handicrafts, perfect for gifts or as a special treat for yourself. Experience the vibrant culture of Ecuador through our products and take home a piece of Andean heritage.
